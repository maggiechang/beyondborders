WHAT WE DO

VOLUNTEER

Once a month, Beyond Borders brings food to the homeless of San Francisco.

UI & HOST DRIVES

We host drives at our high school. Our past drives include food drives and breakfast drives, but in the future, we look forward to hosting winter wear drives.

DELIVER FOOD

Prior to every volunteering event, we pack bagged lunches and/or breakfasts foods for the homeless. Then, we deliver them to San Francisco!

OUR LOCATION

BEYOND BORDERS

One of Burlingame's first volunteering clubs.

  • About

We are a group of high school students eager to help the homeless currently residing in the San Francisco area. Beyond Borders travels into the city of San Francisco and hands out lunch bags and breakfast items for people in need.

Come to our weekly club meetings at lunch in Ms. Crook's classroom, C114!
